About me

I am a PhD student in the department of computer science at Georgetown University, where I am advised by Professor Jeremy T. Fineman. My research interests are graph algorithms and data structures. Before Georgetown, I got my master's degree in computer science at Tufts University and I received my bachelor's degree in mathematics from Boston College.
